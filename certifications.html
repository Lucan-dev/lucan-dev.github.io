Python Essentials 1 by Cisco & Python Institute

March 2024

Completing the Python Essentials 1 course ensures a knowledge of the concepts of computer programming, the syntax and semantics of the Python language as well as demonstrate the ability to accomplish coding tasks related to the essentials of programming in the Python language and resolve implementation challenges using the Python Standard Library.

Cybersafe BC Certified by Justice Education Society & RCMP Foundation

March 2023

Cybersafe BC was developed by the government of Canada. It provides a broad range of information for parents and youth to help them stay safe in the cyber world. Topics include: mobile security risks, protecting your computer, cyber bullying, social networking, and more.

HIGH FIVE Certified by British Columbia Recreation and Parks Association

August 2022

HIGH FIVE is Canada's quality standard for recreation and leisure programs. HIGH FIVE is the only recognized certification for recreation and sport in Canada based on the Five Principles of Healthy Child Development for children aged 4-12 years. HIGH FIVE helps organizations enhance the quality of their children's programs and provide positive experiences for children.

Red Cross Babysitting Certified by Canadian Red Cross

January 2020

The Canadian Red Cross Babysitting course covers everything from managing difficult behaviours to essential content on leadership and professional conduct as a babysitter. It has an emphasis on First Aid skills and giving the appropriate care in the event of an emergency.
