About the Game

Details

There are 60 Discs and 4 Rollers on a VRC Spin Up Field. Discs can be Scored in the two High Goals, one per Alliance, at opposite corners of the field. Each Disc scored in a High Goal is worth 5 points. However, underneath each High Goal, is a 1-point Low Goal for the opposing Alliance.

In addition to Discs, robots can also spin the four Rollers mounted to the field perimeter. If the area inside of a Roller's pointers only shows one color, that is considered “owned” by that Alliance. Each Owned Roller is worth 10 points.

At the end of the match, alliances will receive a 3 point bonus for each Tile their robots are covering.

Scoring

Each Disk scored in High Goal 5 points
Each Disk scored in Low Goal 1 point
Each owned Roller 10 points
Each covered Tile 3 points

Robot

This folding wooden stool is a versatile and convenient piece of furniture that can be used for various purposes. The blend of light and dark brown wood creates a beautiful look. Because of it's foldable design, accurate measuring, marking, and cutting is imperative. To make the joints, I used a drill press to create holes in the wood, inserted wooden dowels inside, and secured it together usign nails. The stool can fold and store easily. It's a simple but functional furniture that serves as a seat or a side table. I enjoyed making this stool and I'm pleased with the result.
