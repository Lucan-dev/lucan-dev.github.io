Frisbot 3.0

Robot

Drivetrain

Our robot consists of 4 subsystems. First, a 6 motor 257rpm drivetrain. It has four omni wheels at the front and back for easy turning and two traction wheels in the middle for stability and center pivoting.

Catapult

The catapult is powered by 1 motor and can fire 66 shots in a minute. Each shot can score 3 discs from up to 6ft away and 2 from 12ft.

Intake and Rollers

We use a single-motor intake that ran at 600 rpm to collect discs, and attached a pair of flex-wheels to the intake with chains to rotate the rollers.

String Shooter

To cover as many tiles as possible at the end of the match our robot uses a string shooting mechanisms. it uses 2 pistons to fire 6 strings across the field for a total of 24 tiles covered.

About the Game

Details

There are 60 Discs and 4 Rollers on a VRC Spin Up Field. Discs can be Scored in the two High Goals, one per Alliance, at opposite corners of the field. Each Disc scored in a High Goal is worth 5 points. However, underneath each High Goal, is a 1-point Low Goal for the opposing Alliance.

In addition to Discs, robots can also spin the four Rollers mounted to the field perimeter. If the area inside of a Roller's pointers only shows one color, that is considered “owned” by that Alliance. Each Owned Roller is worth 10 points.

At the end of the match, alliances will receive a 3 point bonus for each Tile their robots are covering.

Scoring

Each Disc scored in High Goal 5 points
Each Disc scored in Low Goal 1 point
Each owned Roller 10 points
Each covered Tile 3 points
